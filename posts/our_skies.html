Our Skies Above

March 10 2024 8 min read

Mesmerizing, the number one feeling I get when I observe he kosmos above us. Like Clockwork, our Skies motion in as if Earth was it's only audience. As the days progress, I find more and more reasons to believe the space we see above is the canapoy of this earth down below. The mytholoogy of the ancient times spoke of the heavens in similar fashion; as if all the energy of the heavens were concetrated on earth.

What more do we have to discuss in this life? What else is more daring, more in our face then the skies? I believe science has been more propaganda as opposed to it's original purpose: to dissect this realm and anaylze the pieces. However, science is not exclusive. In fact, a universal language for any curious mind to explore.

For Zeus’s mouth does not know how to lie. Each word of his comes true. But you, weigh carefully what you must do, and don’t hold stubbornness above considered judgment.

Observation: Daily, we watch the sun rise and set, almost like a clock. For as long as we have known, the moon only shows us one face, why? and How is this possible? Even more, the sun and moon are positioned just perfectly, rounded in the same size to make a perfect eclipse. Many mysteries of our realm are waiting to be released. This earth, I believe is huge. As far as space drives, as the canopy of the earth, like most mythology describes, the earth is it's companion. I believe there is much more space to earth then a spinning ball sitting in the middle of space. Some days I can see where the earth is infinite. While a sphere can be represented as infinite, I mean earth stretching forever in all directions.

What are those discs in the sky we call planets? I wish I knew or even had a theory. Ancient civilizations called the planets gods over the lives of humans on earth. Why would planets lightyears away even bother to concern themselves with our little old planet? Why do the Astrologers care so much for empty balls so far away? For some reason the moon passes by each planet in relation to earth in a constant cycle. With all the planets circling the sun and earth spinning on it's axis, how is the moon's (a random piece of the earth according to science) travel so anticipated and perfect?

I want to know the truth of our world, of our skies. I am genuinely amazed by the kosmos above us. I am grateful for this experience; I want us to have all the answers and make sense of all of our world to elevate and push forward. Something is distracting the majority of us and life just flies by. Maybe I am living in my imagination, or possibly I am on to something.

ceoDemitri

The world is my imagination. Thank you for being a part of the story. Writing each day to make vision clearer.
