Our Skies Above

Dec 31 2023 11 min read

Since a very young man, a little boy in fact, I would always sense this other way of living. Uncommon was my theory so I kept it balled up and unspoken. Even more, I could rarely synthesize my own thoughts into a comprehensive statement. Today is different. When I look to the skies, I see something calling to be understood. Days go by and the conversations I have with everyday people rarely speak of the magnificence, the magic that sits right above our head, in our face day after day. Mind blowing.

What more do we have to discuss? What else is more daring, more in our face then the skies? I believe science has been more propaganda then anything else. For whatever reason, I don't know and I don't care. All I can concern myself with is what can I do to help and push forward. Daily, we watch the sun rise and set, almost like a clock. For as long as we have known, the moon only shows us one face, why? and How is this possible? Then the moon has the nerve to make faces[phases]?! I need more explanation. The moon is in the perfect space to bounce light from the sun, but half of the earth is positioned just in the right place to be dark?

For Zeus’s mouth does not know how to lie. Each word of his comes true. But you, weigh carefully what you must do, and don’t hold stubbornness above considered judgment.

This earth, I believe is huge. As far as space drives, as the canopy of the earth, like most mythology describes, the earth is it's companion. I believe there is much more space to earth then a spinning ball sitting in the middle of space. Some days I can see where the earth is infinite. While a sphere can be represented as infinite, I mean the earth stretches forever in all directions.

What are those discs in the sky we call planets? I wish I knew or even had a theory. Ancient civilizations called the planets gods over the lives of humans on earth. Why would planets lightyears away even bother to concern themselves with our little old planet? Why do the Astrologers care so much for empty balls so far away? For some reason the moon passes by each planet in relation to earth in a constant cycle. With all the planets circling the sun and earth spinning on it's axis, how is the moon's (a random piece of the earth according to science) travel so anticipated and perfect?

I want to know the truth of our world, of our skies. I am genuinely amazed by the kosmos above us. I am grateful for this experience; I want us to have all the answers and make sense of all of our world. Something is distracting the majority of us and life just flies by. Maybe I am living in my imagination, maybe I am on to something.
